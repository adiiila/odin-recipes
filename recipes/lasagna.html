Mama's Best Lasagna Recipe

The Only Lasagna Recipe You'll Ever Need!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 kg of your choice of ground meat
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 cup feta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly oil a 9x13 baking dish or coat with nonstick spray.
  2. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add your choice of meat and onion and cook until the meat has browned, about 3-5 minutes, making sure to crumble the meat as it cooks; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Drain excess fat. Stir in tomatoes and Italian seasoning until well combined.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine feta, 1/2 cup mozzarella and egg; set aside.
  5. Spread 1 cup tomato mixture onto the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish; top with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/2 of the feta cheese mixture and 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Repeat with a second layer. Top with remaining noodles, tomato mixture, and 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
  6. Place into oven and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until bubbling. Then broil for 2-3 minutes, or until top is browned in spots.
  7. Let cool 15 minutes. Serve, garnished with parsley.
