Baked Sushi Cups

Baked Sushi Cups For The Sushi Lovers!

These delicious baked sushi cups are the best way to get your sushi craving in! Not only are they incredibly cute but so easy to make and pack so much flavor.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces salmon, skin removed and cubed
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tbsp sriracha or sambal
  • 1 1/4 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp kewpie mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • chili powder
  • 3 nori sheets, cut each sheet into 4 squares
  • 1 1/2 cup cooked sushi rice or sticky Jasmine rice
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. To make the spicy mayo, mix together mayo, sambal/sriracha, sesame oil, sugar, and lemon juice in a small bowl. Once it's creamy and smooth, set aside.
  2. To make the salmon, cube it into small individual bites first.
  3. Add mayo, sriracha, lime juice, sesame oil, low sodium soy sauce, and chili powder. Mix together until marinaded evenly. Set aside.
  4. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  5. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons of sticky rice to each nori square. Spread flat. Press into the cupcake tin until the tray is full.
  6. Add about 1 to 2 tablespoons of the salmon on top.
  7. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes. Optional, turn to broil for 2 minutes, give or take, until the top is crispy and golden.
  8. Take out of the oven and sprinkle sesame seeds overtop
