Cheese Fatayer

Cheese Fatayer - A middle eastern savory pastry that will certainly steal the show!

With no special equipment necessary, these cheese pies will fill your home with a delicious aroma. A great addition to the breakfast table, each bite is as aromatic as it is delectable!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Active dry yeast instant yeast will work as well
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups All purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp Kosher salt
  • 1/3 cup Olive oil
  • 3 cups Freshly grated mozzarella (fill up your measuring cup without packing in your cheese)
  • 1 1/4 cups Feta
  • Handful Freshly chopped parsley
  • 4 tbsp Unsalted butter
  • 1/2 tbsp Garlic powder
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Mix together your yeast, sugar, warm water, and flour in a bowl. Cover with a damp paper towel, and allow to sit for 10 minutes.
  2. After 10 minutes, add the rest of your flour (1 1/2 cups), salt, and olive oil to your sponge.
  3. Take a large wooden spoon and roughly combine dough ingredients.
  4. Now, you can use a stand mixer - but if you don't have one, with lightly oiled hands, knead your dough for 8-10 minutes on a clean and oiled work surface until smooth.
  5. Don't add any extra flour to this recipe. The dough will start off quite sticky, but after several minutes of kneading the dough on an oiled work surface - it will eventually come together beautifully.
  6. By limiting the amount of extra flour we add to the dough, we will be able to create a very fluffy, as well as airy bread for our cheese pies.
  7. Next, separate your dough into 10 dough balls (each around 62-65 grams in weight). Cover with a damp towel and allow to rest for 10 minutes.
  8. After 10 minutes, lightly sprinkle flour on your work surface. Roll out each dough ball about a half an inch thick.
  9. Now its time to build the crust. Add a light amount of freshly grated mozzarella to the edges of your dough ( just plain mozzarella, not the filling we made). Start folding your dough over the mozzarella, pressing down to seal the edges. Watch video above for guidance.
  10. Fold the edges of your crust into a diamond shape, pinching the top and bottom of your dough to enforce the shape even more.
  11. Preheat oven to 425°f (218°C)
  12. Add your stuffed, and shaped dough to a lined baking sheet. Brush egg wash on your crust, and brush garlic butter in the center of your dough. Fill cheese pies with the cheese filling.
  13. Bake for 25-30 minutes. Every oven preforms differently, keep a close eye on them. You want the bottom of your pies, as well as your crust, to be beautifully golden.
